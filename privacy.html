We collect only the necessary data to provide our vehicle marketplace services, including contact information for sellers and buyers, and technical identifiers for security.

2. Your Rights under GDPR

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is limited to authorized personnel only.
